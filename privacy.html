Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Cameron (“App”) by Afternoon Products, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data to provide core functionality:

  • Photos & Images: Images you upload are processed for AI features but are not stored on our servers.
  • Account Information: We require sign-in using your Apple account. We do not store personal information like name, email, or Apple ID.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power certain features:

  • OpenAI API: Processes your text and image inputs to generate AI responses.
  • Analytics Providers: May collect technical usage data (e.g., device type, app interactions) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Uploaded images stay on your device. Temporary processing occurs via third-party APIs but no images are retained after processing.

Analytics data is anonymized and does not personally identify users.

4. Data Security

We apply standard security practices to protect data in transit. For third-party services, please review their individual security policies. No system can guarantee 100% security.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. AI Output Disclaimer

AI-generated responses are based on user inputs and may contain speculative or inaccurate information. Outputs are not stored or linked to individual users.

7. User Rights

Since we do not collect personal identifying information, requests to access, correct, or delete such data are generally not applicable. For questions regarding third-party services, refer to their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
