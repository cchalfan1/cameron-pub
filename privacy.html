Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Cameron (“App”) by Afternoon Products, LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data We Collect

We strive to collect as little personal data as possible. Specifically:

  • Photos & Images: Images you upload are processed for AI responses but are not stored on our servers.
  • Account Required: We do require registration, using your apple account. We do not store any personal information.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate certain features:

  • OpenAI API: Processes your text and image inputs to generate AI responses.

These services may collect technical data (e.g., device type, usage metrics) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Uploaded images remain on your device and are not stored by us. Temporary processing may occur via the OpenAI API, but we do not retain image data after processing.

Usage analytics data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security measures to protect the integrity of the App and data in transit. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

For data processed by third-party services, please review their respective security policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Cameron is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. AI Output Disclaimer

AI responses are generated based on inputs and may produce speculative or inaccurate content. Outputs are not stored or associated with individual users.

7. User Rights

Since we do not collect personal identifying information, requests to access, correct, or delete personal data are generally not applicable. For questions regarding third-party services, please refer to their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
